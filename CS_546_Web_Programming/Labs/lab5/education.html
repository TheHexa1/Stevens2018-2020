Where did I get my training?

Initial training: Konoha Ninja Academy

Favourite class: Iruka sensei's class

Memorble event:
Failing once again to graduate from the Academy, a disappointed me was advised by one of my instructors, Mizuki, to steal the Scroll of Seals and learn a technique from it in order to graduate. As I struggled to learn the Shadow Clone Technique, Iruka Umino tracked me down and realised that I was tricked by Mizuki into stealing the scroll. Mizuki attacked us and told me about the Nine-Tails sealed within me, claiming that Iruka hated me because of it. When Iruka risked his life to protect me, however, I saw through Mizuki's lies and used the Multiple Shadow Clone Technique to defeat him, prompting Iruka to happily grant me my graduation from the Academy. 😁

Second training: Under Master Jiraiya

Favourite place: Mount Byoku(Where sage toad lives)

Memorble event:
Two years into our training, My control of the Nine-Tails' chakra had improved significantly. Jiraiya tried to give me more access to the Nine-Tails' power and uses Gerotora to weaken my Eight Trigrams Sealing Style. Seizing the opportunity, the Nine-Tails mocked me for my inability to save Sasuke, using my negative emotions to force me to enter a version 2 form with four tails. Not in control of my body, I attacked Jiraiya and nearly killed him before he manages to suppress the Nine-Tails. I had no memory of what happened and Jiraiya told me afterwards. He also told me to keep my anger in check so that the Nine-Tails won't flare up again. 😨

Jinchuriki control training: Under Killer Bee (8 tail's Jinchuriki)

Favourite place: On the back of floating Turtle size of an island

Memorble event:
While I was doing tug-of-war with nine tails to control its Chakra, just before I was completely consumed, the spirit of my mother, Kushina Uzumaki, appeared. I believed that Kushina was the Nine-Tails in disguise, earning a hit on the head and an immediately apology from her, who hoped that I did not inherit her short-temper. Realising Kushina was my mother, I tearfully hugged her and the love-filled reunion purged the Nine-Tails' hatred within its drained chakra. With renewed confidence from hearing the story of my parents, I battled the Nine-Tails once more. With my mother's assistance, I successfully weakened the fox long enough to separate it from its chakra. Attaining the Nine-Tails Chakra Mode, I imprisoned the emaciated and infuriated Nine-Tails within a new, stronger seal, apologising to it as it faded into darkness. Afterwards, I learned from Kushina about my heritage, the truth behind the Nine-Tails' attack on Konoha, and how my parents gave their lives to protect me. I told my mother that I could finally understood what a parent's love felt like and that I didn't blame them for what happened, instead feeling glad to be their son. As she faded away, Kushina tearfully hugged Naruto, thanking him for letting her and Minato be his parents. 😟 😢 😭
